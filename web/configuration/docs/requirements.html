Requirements

To use this framework nothing special is required:
  • A Webserver (Apache or NGinX or IIS)
  • PHP 5.4 or greater (maybe HHVM-Equivalent)
  • due to YOUR needs some PHP-Modules/HHVM-Modules
  • due to YOUR needs the Database (currently supported: PGSQL, MySQL - be free to develop own ones)
thats it
